About

I have always had a passion for art at a young age. My favorite past-time was making creations with pencil and paper. As I grew older my hobbies of sketches and drawings transformed to creating blueprints of items I would like to make. My passion for creating evolved into wanting to learn new languages (programming that is) for developing websites and mobile applications.

Some of the languages that I know are Java, HTML/CSS, PHP, Javascript, Python, XML, Bootstrap, and Angular JS. I have helped build a web applications for USDA and mobile application for Metropolitan State University. I am perusing my journey as a developer.
